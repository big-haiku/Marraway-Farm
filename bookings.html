Looking for your next getaway?

Check out our two five-star rooms, located deep in the heart of Tasmania's beautiful Tamar Valley. Enjoy the rural luxury of a stately home hosted by an Airbnb certified superhost.

The Kookaburra Room

Check out the Kookaburra Room on Airbnb This spacious suite boasts a light and airy environment, complete with fantastic views of our verdant woodland. With a queen bed, walk in wardrobe, fully-tiled ensuite, heating and cooling systems, a smart tv with access to Netflix and Plex, and Wifi access, the Kookaburra Room has everything you need for a perfect retreat. Both of our rooms have access to a dining room - where coffee, tea, and more, is offered for your convenience - and comfortable lounge and outdoor veranda so you can enjoy the warmth of our fireplace in winter, or the warmth of the sun in summer.

The Woodland Room

Check out the Woodland Room on Airbnb The Woodland Room offers you a return to traditional rustic living without sacrificing the comforts of modern life. With a cosy queen bed, ensuite, heating and cooling systems, smart tv with access to Netflix and Plex, and Wifi access, your stay in the Woodland Room is sure to be a homely one. Wake up to great views of the Tamar Valley's lush woodland and enjoy our hospitality as we provide access to a dining room - where coffee, tea, and more, is offered for your convenience - and comfortable lounge and outdoor veranda so you can enjoy the warmth of our fireplace in winter, or the warmth of the sun in summer.
