What is Marraway Farm?

Marraway Farm is a rural property seated in the heart of Tasmania's famous Tamar Valley. Surrounded by great vineyards, breathtaking nature trails, and vibrant and friendly communities, Marraway Farm is perfectly positioned to play host to your next adventures through our 5 star rooms, offered on Airbnb.

Marraway Farm is also home to a number of sheep - which guests to our Airbnb can meet and get to know - as well as a wide array of Tasmanian wildlife. Our property is home to bandicoots, echidnas, wallabies, padmelons, wombats, possums, parrots, and more, and you're very likely to see them.

Marraway Farm also represents an expanding enterprise. We plan to offer a range of lovingly designed and crafted products, showcasing the beauty offered by Tasmania and its wildlife to local stores and to you directly through our store. Our mission is to showcase the very best of what Tasmania has to offer, both in terms of hospitality and the natural world.
